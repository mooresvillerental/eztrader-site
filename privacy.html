V73 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

V73 is an AI-assisted trading signal app operated by V73 Technologies LLC. The app is designed to help users monitor trade setups, portfolio/session information, signal status, diagnostics, and alerts.

This Privacy Policy explains what information V73 may collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

V73 may collect or process the following information:

App Functionality Data

The app may request and display portfolio/session values, trade signal data, trade history, shadow performance statistics, and signal diagnostic information through the V73 backend.

Notification Data

V73 uses push notifications to deliver trade setup alerts. To support this feature, the app may collect and send an Expo push notification token to the V73 backend.

User Actions

When a user confirms, denies, clears, resets, or updates app/session information, those actions may be sent to the V73 backend so the app can function properly.

Local App Storage

V73 may store certain alert/session information locally on the device so that alerts and app state can persist when the app is closed and reopened.

2. How We Use Information

V73 uses collected information only for app functionality, including:

  • Displaying portfolio/session information
  • Showing trade signal status
  • Delivering push notifications and alerts
  • Saving alert state
  • Confirming or denying trade setup actions
  • Resetting or updating session information
  • Improving app reliability and diagnostics

3. What We Do Not Collect

V73 does not intentionally collect:

  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • Photos or videos
  • Audio recordings
  • Calendar data
  • Health data
  • Browsing history

4. Advertising and Tracking

V73 does not use collected information for third-party advertising or ad targeting.

5. Data Sharing

V73 does not sell user data.

Data may be processed through service providers necessary for app functionality, such as backend hosting, app build services, push notification services, and related infrastructure providers.

6. Data Security

V73 uses HTTPS when communicating with its backend services. While reasonable steps are taken to protect data, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Trading Disclaimer

V73 provides informational trading signals and app-based monitoring tools. V73 does not guarantee profits and does not place trades automatically. Trading involves risk, and users are responsible for their own trading decisions.

8. Children’s Privacy

V73 is not intended for children. The app is intended for users who are old enough to make independent financial decisions and understand trading risk.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised effective date.
